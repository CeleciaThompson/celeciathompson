Summary of the Twelve Pillars
    It is powerful to write goals because when it is written it is then brought to reality.Your brain will automatically remember those goals and push you towards them which will lead to you eventually achieving them. To set your goals the first step is to write it down. There are four types of goals according to Mr. Davis, there is long-term goal, mid-range goal, short-range goal and micro-goals; even though the length of each goal is different each goal leads you to the next.
    It is also said that time management is important because time goes by fast. This means when you might think you do not have time, you do because everyday is another other opportunity so spend each day wisely. In order to be successful it is important to surround yourself with persons that are strong minded and has the same goal as you do. This is so because every relationship you have is an association, and it has either a positive, neutral or negative effect on you. It is advised that you categorize each person that you associate with because in order to be successful you have to hang around successful persons to gain that sense of competition to be a better version of yourself and to have a better attitude.
    However, in order to be successful you have to be a life long learner because learning does not stop at formal education, it goes a long way. In order to continue learning you have to read books, observe successful persons and learn from your experiences.
    I will definitely apply these lessons to my life because it truly opened my eyes and taught me a lot. I will absolutely categorize my friends and spend my time wise because it goes by quickly. This means that I will spend less time discussing less important things with friends, procastinating and go ahead and associate with persons that are in the same mind set as I am. I will continue to work towards my short term goals that were set for this year and go ahead with writing my mid-term and long term goals because I want to feel the power when those goals are achieved.
